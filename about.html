About FOOD&RECIPE

Welcome! I'm happy you're here.You should know that I'm totally obsessed with food.I daydream about new recipes. I devour cookbooks. I read up on food policy and nutrition.I love photographing food and hope my photos inspire you to step into the kitchen to cook a delicious,healthy meal.

I believe that cooking should be fun and recipes,flexible.I choose not to cook meat,so all the recipes on FOOD&RECIPE are vegetarian. I believe in eating whole foods,which are foods that are as close to their source as possible.

"This website is all about celebrating good food-real,sustainable food that delights the senses and nourishes the body."
